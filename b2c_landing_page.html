Zašto Odmah

Why Right Now

Tvoje Tijelo Ne Čeka. Your Body Won't Wait.

Istraživanja sportske nutricionistike pokazuju jasno: timing proteina nije mit — to je biokemija. Sports nutrition research is clear: protein timing isn't a myth — it's biochemistry.

3×
Brža sinteza proteina
Faster protein synthesis

Uzimanje 20–40g proteina unutar 45 min nakon treninga povećava mišićnu sintezu proteina do 3× više nego čekanje 2+ sata. Razlog: povišeni kortizol + mišićna glikogenoliza maksimiziraju GLUT-4 aktivnost. Taking 20–40g of protein within 45 min post-training increases muscle protein synthesis up to 3× compared to waiting 2+ hours. Reason: elevated cortisol + muscle glycogenolysis maximise GLUT-4 activity.

Ivy JL, Sports Medicine 2004; Levenhagen DK, Am J Physiol 2001

⚡
Inzulinska osjetljivost — vrhunac odmah
Insulin sensitivity — peak right now

Odmah nakon treninga mišićne stanice su maksimalno otvorene za glukozu i aminokiseline — kao prozor koji se zatvara. Protein + mala količina ugljikohidrata u tom prozoru maximizira anabolički odgovor i smanjuje katabolizam. Right after training, muscle cells are maximally open to glucose and amino acids — like a window that's closing. Protein + small carbs within that window maximises anabolic response and reduces catabolism.

Tipton KD, J Nutr 2001; Burke LM, Int J Sport Nutr 2003

🥤
Tekući protein — brža apsorpcija
Liquid protein — faster absorption

Tekući whey protein dostiže vrhunac apsorpcije već za 60–90 minuta — brže od čvrstih izvora proteina koji zahtijevaju probavu. Svježe mješanje bez konzervansa čuva integritet aminokiselinskog profila. Liquid whey protein peaks absorption within 60–90 minutes — faster than solid protein sources that require digestion. Fresh mixing without preservatives preserves the amino acid profile integrity.

Boirie Y, PNAS 1997; Tang JE, J Appl Physiol 2009

🌱
Vegan opcija — isti rezultat
Vegan option — same results

Miješani biljni proteini (grašak + riža) postižu usporediv anabolički odgovor s wheyem ako dostignu 2–3g leucina po obroku. Shake-O-Mat vegan recept je formuliran prema tim kriterijima. Mixed plant proteins (pea + rice) achieve comparable anabolic response to whey when reaching 2–3g leucine per serving. Shake-O-Mat's vegan recipe is formulated to those criteria.

van Vliet S, J Nutr 2015; Babault N, JISSN 2015
