Česta Pitanja

Frequently Asked Questions

Imate Pitanja?

Got Questions?

Koliko košta postavljanje automata? How much does machine placement cost?

Teretana plaća jednokratno 3.000€ za postavljanje i aktivaciju automata (tržišna vrijednost opreme: €5.000+). Nakon toga Shake-O-Mat preuzima sve troškove: servisiranje, punjenje kanistera i logistiku — teretana samo prima prihod od revenue share modela.

The gym pays a one-time €3,000 for machine installation and activation (market value of equipment: €5,000+). After that, Shake-O-Mat covers all costs — servicing, restocking, and logistics. The gym simply receives its revenue share.

Kako se dijeli prihod? How is revenue split?

Prihod se dijeli prema tier modelu baziranom na prometu vaše teretane:

Starter (do 200 članova): 10% od svake prodaje
Growth (200–500 članova): 20% od svake prodaje
Partner (500+ članova): 25% od svake prodaje

Ako vaše osoblje preuzima punjenje i redovno održavanje automata, dobivate +5% bonusa na vaš tier — dakle do 30% ukupno. Transparentni cloud obračun; isplata jednom mj. na tekući račun.

Revenue is shared based on a traffic tier model:

Starter (up to 200 members): 10% of every sale
Growth (200–500 members): 20% of every sale
Partner (500+ members): 25% of every sale

If your staff handles restocking and routine maintenance, you receive a +5% bonus on your tier — up to 30% total. Transparent cloud accounting; monthly payment to your business account.

Tko puni i servisira automat? Who restocks and services the machine?

Isključivo Shake-O-Mat tim. Vaše osoblje ne dira automat — mi pratimo zalihe kroz cloud sustav i dolazimo na punjenje prema potrebi. Čišćenje i preventivno održavanje također naša obaveza.

Exclusively the Shake-O-Mat team. Your staff never touches the machine — we monitor stock via cloud and restock as needed. Cleaning and preventive maintenance are also our responsibility.

Koliki prostor zauzima automat? How much space does the machine take?

JK91 dimenzije su 76cm x 67cm x 181cm (Š x D x V). Potrebna je strujnja utičnica (230V). Optimalna lokacija: recepcija ili prostor između svlačionica i sale.

JK91 dimensions are 76cm x 67cm x 181cm (W x D x H). A standard power outlet (230V) is required. Optimal placement: reception area or between changing rooms and gym floor.

Mogu li prekinuti suradnju? Can I end the partnership?

Da — s otkaznim rokom definiranim ugovorom o suradnji (standardno 30-60 dana). Automat ostaje vaš (platili ste 3.000€), ali možemo dogovoriti otkup opreme ili nastavak s drugačijim uvjetima. Bez penalizacije za teretanu.

Yes — with a notice period defined in the partnership agreement (typically 30-60 days). The machine stays yours (you paid €3,000 for it), but we can arrange an equipment buyback or new terms. No penalties for the gym.

Koje teretane su prikladne? Which gyms are suitable?

Fitness centri, teretane, CrossFit boxovi, wellness studiji, sportski centri s 100+ aktivnih članova. Procjenjujemo svaku lokaciju individualno — kontaktirajte nas za besplatnu procjenu.

Fitness centres, gyms, CrossFit boxes, wellness studios, sports centres with 100+ active members. We assess every location individually — contact us for a free assessment.
